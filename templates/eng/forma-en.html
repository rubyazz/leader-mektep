UNIFORM

Uniform

Wearing a school uniform is a symbol of pride, a unique feature of the school and an integral part of being a student. We hope that wearing school uniforms will make students feel safe at school, as well as prevent social inequality. According to the results of the study, wearing a uniform at school is the reason for the following positive results:

  • The absence and delay of classes are reduced

  • The difficulty decreases with discipline

  • The number of school dropouts decreases

  • Improving academic performance
