    EDUCATIONAL WORK

    In primary school, we try to create a caring environment for our students and create conditions for students and parents to learn together.We want to try to eliminate all possible obstacles to our students, to encourage them to acquire modern knowledge and build their abilities.

    Within the framework of the primary school education system, we monitor and regulate school discipline through the incentive system. In this system, students earn appropriate points for the positive actions they perform. In addition, during educational hours, our teachers conduct classes in accordance with the content of the educational program.

    Our educational program provides for the implementation of the following goals:

    • Students' good understanding of themselves and others

    • Mastering the necessary abilities to build and maintain relationships

    • Training proper self-esteem

    • Formation of the appropriate values and moral code in life

    • Mastering the skills necessary to make decisions and be confident

    • Learning to lead a healthy lifestyle
