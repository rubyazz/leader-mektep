    SELF-MANAGEMENT WORK

    Self-management work

    In our Lyceum, student leadership is an important part of the educational process. We believe that every student has the potential to lead formally or informally. Our goal is to provide students with an understanding of ethical and effective leadership, as well as to evaluate and develop their abilities to become a confident leader in the future.The purpose of the council is to work closely with the school administration, staff and parents for the common good of the school community.

    Activities of the student council:

    • Expressing the views of schoolchildren to the school administration;

    • Encourage close relationships within the school;

    • Contributing to the progress and development of students;

    • Promoting the adaptation of new students to school;

    • Participation in the organization of sports and cultural events of the school;

    • Organization of targeted charity events;

    • Establish partnerships with self-government boards in other schools.
