    Philosophy

    We aim to improve the academic and social abilities of students. We strive to increase the responsibility of our students for the surrounding environment.

    The goal of our curriculum is to maintain a balance between the desire for a strong academic foundation and the psychological support that leads our students to become active apprentices, relying on independent thinking, behavioral development and emotional stability. In order to become a Global Citizen, our students must support and accept such concepts as intercultural understanding, tolerance and respect.
