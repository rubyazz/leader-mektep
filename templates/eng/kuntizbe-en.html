    ANNUAL CALENDAR

    Annual calendar

    Taking into account the requirements of the school community and the international program, the school calendar plan is divided into 4 quarters covers 33 weeks and begins the first week of September and ends in the first week of June. 2022-2023 academic year the following period:

    • 1st quarter: from September 1, 2022 to October 28, 2022 (9 weeks)

    • 2nd quarter: from November 7, 2022 to December 29, 2022 (8 weeks)

    • 3rd quarter: from January 9, 2023 to March 17, 2023 (6 weeks)

    • 4th quarter: from March 27, 2023 to June 1, 2023 (10 weeks)

    You can see the quarterly holidays and official weekends of the 2022-2023 academic year in our annual calendar.
